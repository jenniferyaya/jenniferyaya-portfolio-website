What cruel fate of man, to be made to love. We feel love in spite of death — it’s pending arrival and its oppressive finality. Mortal memories that will disappear one day. Mortal memories that carry the weight of the known universe. I write them down, speedily, to capture all of love's painful, glorious, all-encompassing feeling. Difficult to define and so strongly felt.

That is the most painful thing. It is the most beautiful thing.

Flying across the Saharan Desert: The uniform and wise landscape of the desert, where the simpler, more stoic parts of me find a home.
