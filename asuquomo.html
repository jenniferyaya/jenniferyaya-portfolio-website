I collaborated with multidisciplinary artist Asuquomo to develop high-quality imagery and designs for his concept album, Wide Awake. Drawing from his own very informed visual aesthetic, I created striking visuals that matched the kinetic energy of the accompanying video. These images highlighted the artist’s physicality and showcased this through different designs.
