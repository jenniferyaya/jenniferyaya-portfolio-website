Travel To-Do List

Dreaming just a little bit, thinking ahead to adventuring

Some stories I would like to chase:

Visit the Salar de Uyuni in Bolivia during the dry season

Spend a day in Cala Iris, Morocco. Just because of its beautiful name.

Live by the beach in Biarritz for a whole week and enroll in surfing lessons each day.

Spend a weekend in Wales, buy all the best books from the loveliest bookshops.

Swim all day in Lefkáda, Greece.

Take the 7-hour train journey from Bergen to Oslo, and vice versa.

Camp in the Sahara.

Camp for a few days under the Rwenzori Mountains and climb or hike what you can.

Visit Santiago de Cuba during it's Carnaval festival.

Reread Botchan while in Matsuyama, Japan, taking the book with me everywhere.

Visit Ras Kebdana, take photos and swim.

Celebrate New Years in Florianopolis and Moscow.

Revisit Dar es Salaam, then take a day trip to Kidoti.

Explore as much of Portugal in particular, Porto Moniz and its natural swimming pools.

Visit Bruges in the summer and go to a music festival.

Go to Scotland a few times and make sure to visit The Isle of Skye.

Go to Deià, Majorca.

Relax in Isla Holbox, Quintana Roo.

Take an outdoor shower somewhere quiet and tropical.
